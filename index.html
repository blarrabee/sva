Service

Each student participates in community service projects with our existing partners such as SPCA, Habitat for Humanity, Wildlife Rescue, San Jose Women’s shelter, and Big Brothers/Big Sisters. Additionally, they can determine other options with their mentor that will suit their interests and talents.

Work Placement

Students at SVA can choose from a wide variety of pre-established relationships with companies such as Facebook, Google, Box Networks, Zenga, Electronic Arts, Tesla, and many more. Conversely, they can work with their mentor to identify other opportunities, and carve their own path. With a 4% unemployment rate in the Silicon Valley, you are setting yourself up for endless opportunities. Mentors are there to help design and educate students with the necessary resume/cover letter writing skills, and vocabulary to prepare one for an interview.

Student Advocacy

Situated centrally, in Palo Alto, SVA is minutes away from Stanford University, University of Santa Clara, Foothill and De Anza (ranked best Community colleges in California), and Palo Alto University. Participants of SVA will have the opportunity to enroll and pursue classes of their interests and passions. While enrolled in the program, participants will be assisted with Executive Functioning, organization, leadership, and study skills. In addition, participants will receive the coaching necessary to establish new habits for sleep, health and nutrition, and stress relief/tolerance

Staying Healthy

An active lifestyle is at the heart of the SVA program. There is no better place than the Silicon Valley to assist in this active lifestyle. Bicycling, rock-climbing, surfing, Mountain Biking, Gym memberships are just a few of the many activities available in the area.
